4Coach/Instructor

When you enter a coach who is a USMS member, it will display their USMS-Certified Coach or ALTS Instructor credentials below and render those badges on the club detail page, which help you achieve the Gold Club designation. If your coach is a non-member, they must have a My USMS login to appear in the dropdown. If they don't appear in the dropdown, you can help them create a My USMS login using the "Add a Coach" button below and an account creation email will be sent to them. You can enter as many coaches as you want. If you have multiple coaches who are USMS members, we recommend you select a Head Coach.
